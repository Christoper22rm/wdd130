Ecuador

Ecuador, a vibrant country located on the northwest coast of South America, is known for its incredible biodiversity, breathtaking landscapes and rich cultural heritage. From the Galapagos Islands to the Andes and the Amazon, Ecuador offers a unique variety of natural and cultural experiences. In addition to the famous Galapagos Islands, Ecuador is home to an incredible variety of natural landscapes including tropical beaches, active volcanoes such as Cotopaxi, and a dense Amazon jungle teeming with wildlife.
